What We Stand For

Our Vision & Mission

Project Vitality was founded at Sri Venkateswara College (SVC) to solve a critical health gap: college students neglect physical health due to irregular routines, poor food availability, and lack of biomarker tracking.

Our core mission is to design and implement a sustainable, student-led health initiative that improves actual student biomarkers (lipid profiles, blood sugar, stress metrics), rather than focusing solely on superficial, gym-bro physical aesthetics.

By combining behavioral science, seasonal mapping, digital audits, and local partnerships, we aim to institutionalize wellness as a standard component of Delhi University campus life.

The Realities & Constraints

We operate as a student-led organization with zero institutional funding, meaning all initiatives must be filtered through a strict feasibility lens:

1. Zero Budget All operations must rely on zero cash outlay. Prizes are sourced as coupons/promotions from local cafes and printing services.
2. Limited Volunteer Hours Volunteers are full-time students with lectures, assignments, and exams. Initiatives must require minimal maintenance time.
3. Cultural Resistance Traditional wellness is viewed as "uncool" or preachy. Campaigns must use gamification, humor, and digital-first delivery to build engagement.

The Three Strategy Pillars

Our operational model is divided into three key verticals to balance awareness, direct behavioral impact, and data gathering.

📢

Awareness

Essence: Low-barrier cognitive framing. Shifting wellness from a preachy chore to a performance-oriented "biohack," requiring nothing but passive attention.

Capturing attention, educating, and altering student mindsets without requiring active behavioral changes.

  • Digital Canteen Guide (1.1): Crowdsourced mobile food metrics and high-protein recommendations.
  • Mythbusters Series (1.2): Meme-based carousels debunking campus wellness myths.
  • Vitality Micro-Broadcasts (1.3): 15-minute Instagram/Zoom live Q&As with health experts.
  • Dorm Room Hacks Videos (1.4): Fast-paced mobile Reels detailing cheap circadian and sleep hacks.
  • Nutrition Decoders (1.5): Translating packaged snack sugars and sodium into visual equivalents.
  • Seminars with Industry Experts (1.6): Live physical expert talks coordinated directly during class times.
⚡

Impact

Essence: Active behavioral conversion. Driving direct updates to student biomarkers by negotiating institutional food swaps or organizing gamified challenges.

Implementing structured, actionable interventions on campus that demand active student participation or environment upgrades.

  • Canteen Condition Analysis (2.1): Auditing current canteen prep and offering zero-cost menu upgrades backed by student petitions.
  • On-Campus Biomarker Health Camp (2.2): Low-cost biomarker testing camp operated entirely by external lab clinical staff.
  • VenkyWalks Step Challenge (2.3): Departmental walking leagues during winter with AQI-safety fallback.
  • Impromptu Challenges (2.4): Live push-up/pull-up break activations with immediate sponsored tokens.
🔬

Research

Essence: Empirical proof-of-concept. Gathering localized metrics to build a database, providing the leverage to lobby administrators.

Conducting academic and analytical study frameworks throughout the year to understand the college health baseline and revise policies.

  • Annual Health Census (3.1): Anonymous 3-minute Google Form mapping student sleep, diet, and fatigue.
  • PG Housing Diet Audits (3.2): Mobile diet diaries surveying off-campus tiffin provider deficits.
  • Reference-Based Food Estimates (3.3): USDA/IFCT recipe audits mapping canteen staples.
